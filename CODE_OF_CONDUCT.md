# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

We want Frontier Graph to be a respectful, constructive project for researchers, developers, and curious users.

Examples of behavior that contribute to a positive environment:

- being respectful in disagreement
- giving actionable, specific feedback
- assuming good intent while still being honest
- keeping criticism focused on ideas, code, evidence, and product behavior

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- harassment or personal attacks
- abusive or demeaning language
- discriminatory behavior
- deliberate bad-faith disruption of discussion or review

## Scope

This applies in GitHub issues, pull requests, discussions around the repository, and project-related communication spaces tied to Frontier Graph.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject comments, issues, or contributions that do not follow this code of conduct.

Serious or repeated violations may lead to temporary or permanent exclusion from project spaces.

## Reporting

If you experience or observe behavior that violates this code of conduct, contact:
