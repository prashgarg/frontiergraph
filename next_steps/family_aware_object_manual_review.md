# Family-Aware Object Manual Review

## Scope

This review looks at the strengthened `family_aware` prototype after the second reviewed family-seeding pass.

Current coverage:

- `24` prototype rows
- `12` unique pairs after collapsing horizon duplicates

The question is whether family-aware objects are now strong enough to promote beyond prototype status.

## Overall verdict

Family-aware objects are now **real** and **useful**, but they are still best treated as a **secondary frontier object**, not the main paper-facing object.

Why:

- the stronger reviewed family layer clearly improved the object
- the best examples now read as legitimate underexplored-within-family questions
- but the family-aware wording still tends to sound diagnostic unless the pair has an obvious sibling comparison in mind

So this object has moved from "underpowered" to "credible secondary object."

## Strong examples

### Macro cycle and price family

These are the strongest non-environment examples:

- `state of the business cycle -> house prices`
- `state of the business cycle -> rate of growth`
- `state of the business cycle -> price changes`
- `state of the business cycle -> inflation`

Why they work:

- the family is coherent
- the concepts are related but not mergeable
- the question naturally invites a comparison against adjacent macro outcomes

This is close to a real metascience object:
"within this family of macro outcomes, what is specific to this pair rather than a general family-level association?"

### Innovation and technology family

Also strong:

- `technological innovation -> digital economy`
- `digital economy development -> digital economy`
- `technological innovation -> technological progress`

Why they work:

- the family is coherent and contemporary
- the pairs naturally raise the issue of whether the relation is specific or merely a broad innovation-family blur

These read better than the earlier environmental-only family-aware objects.

## Mixed or weaker examples

### Environmental family

Examples such as:

- `CO2 emissions -> ecological footprint`
- `environmental pollution -> environmental quality`
- `environmental pollution -> ecological footprint`

are useful, but they still often read like:

- ontology-boundary checks
- family-structure diagnostics
- or substantive outcome-boundary clarifications

That does not make them bad.
It just means they are often more useful for ontology reasoning than for paper-facing frontier presentation.

## Main limitation

The current phrasing is still too generic:

- "what is specific to the X -> Y link?"

This is directionally right, but it does not yet say:

- specific relative to **which siblings**
- specific in **what dimension**
- or what comparison a researcher should actually make next

That is why the object is not yet strong enough to replace path/mechanism or routed context/evidence objects.

## Promotion decision

### Promote now

Promote family-aware objects to:

- internal review packs
- ontology-vNext working notes
- paper-adjacent methodology discussion

### Do not promote yet

Do not yet promote family-aware objects to:

- the main paper object
- the default routed shortlist layer

## Recommended next improvement

The next improvement should be **phrasing**, not family breadth alone.

The right next template should compare the pair against sibling concepts explicitly, for example:

- "Within the macro cycle and price family, is the business-cycle link stronger for house prices than for inflation or growth?"
- "Within the innovation and technology family, what is specific to the digital-economy link relative to adjacent innovation outcomes?"

That would turn the object from a diagnostic question into a more actionable comparative frontier question.

## Bottom line

Family-aware objects are now good enough to keep and iterate.

They are no longer thin because of missing family structure.
They are now limited mostly by:

- wording
- comparator specificity
- and uneven family maturity across domains

So the correct move is:

- keep them active as a secondary ontology-vNext object
- improve family-aware phrasing next
- and only then reconsider whether they belong in the main paper-facing surface
