Method note

How FrontierGraph works

FrontierGraph is built around a narrow rule: AI may help extract graph structure from papers, but the ranking itself should remain deterministic and inspectable.

Pipeline

  1. Convert papers into a directed claim graph.
  2. Enumerate missing links implied by underexplored pairs, paths, and motifs.
  3. Score those links with transparent graph signals and penalties.
  4. Let users inspect mediators, paths, and supporting papers.

What drives the ranking

  • underexplored concept-pair gaps
  • path-implied missing direct edges
  • motif completion in open triads
  • field distance, prior contact, and hub penalties

What the beta is not doing yet

  • saved agendas or accounts
  • downstream value forecasting
  • full graph-of-science coverage
  • a custom domain for the app itself
