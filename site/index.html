What it is for

A fast first pass on where the literature may be leaving room.

FrontierGraph is meant for researchers who want a structured shortlist rather than a black-box answer. The interface is intentionally compact: shortlist first, evidence second.

Why trust it

  • AI extracts the graph. It does not decide the ranking logic.
  • Each suggestion can be inspected through mediators, paths, and papers.
  • The current beta is focused on being legible before it tries to be broad.
